<h3 className="mb-0">How to <span className="text-red">create processes that won't drive you crazy</span></h3>

Some of the tools we use, like GitHub as a CMS, may sound intimidating to non-engineers, but [our experience](/blog/a-non-coders-thoughts-on-everybody-codes-culture) is that if you give teams the right support then it isn’t an issue. It helps to have [a really good employee handbook](/handbook).
